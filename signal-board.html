02

Who the reel talks to

Austin monetizes a funnel, not just an audience. So the reels use Kallaway's shape but aim at his buyer, not just the biggest number. Two overlapping people:

The Viewer · reach

The AI-native operator

25-45, founder / consultant / ambitious exec. Uses Claude daily, not an engineer. Wants leverage and "the exact system"; fears being "not technical enough" and "behind." Watches, shares, buys the $7 skills product + Build Partner.

↳ pull broadly · tactical + demo-able
The Buyer · the money

The non-technical founder / exec

Runs a real business, has budget, won't become a developer. Wants AI installed in ops, a "pseudo fractional COO." Trusts proof Austin has actually operated: the $20M-COO story, JPMorgan, the $400K-app receipt. Funds the executive AI program.

↳ seed the worldview · differentiate on receipts
Reel → newsletter / $7 skills → Build Partner → executive AI program (the money)
↳ the reel's job: pull the viewer broadly, seed the buyer's worldview so the funnel fills underneath

The sharpening: frame every tactic as an outcome an operator cares about (leverage, decisions, ops, cost), not a dev trick · lead some hooks on the fear, not just the desire · bias to "shown, not told."  Selection rule for the 4: ~2 builder-reach (A1, A2, A7, A3) + ~1 operator-bridge (B1, B2, A8, C3) + 1 wildcard.
