How it would work each month

The same rhythm each month. AI does the grunt work; we spend the time on the writing.

1

Ideas

  • 5 ideas a week, mined from your videos
  • Framed through what's trending and in the news right now
  • Builds your pipeline: an idea bank months deep over time
2

Pick three

  • You pick the week's three
  • Riff on what you'd actually say
  • Your words become the material
3

I write it

  • The hook, locked first
  • The structure and ordering
  • Rewritten until it sounds like you, your riff notes in
  • Every prompt and quote sourced
4

Storyboard

  • Shot by shot, with my notes on how it should look
  • Timing included
  • You film and edit
5

Your final pass

  • The last voice pass is yours, then I hand it off
  • Notes back in 48h, two revision rounds each
6

Monthly review

  • We read the numbers: which hooks held, which topics pulled
  • It all feeds back into the system
12 scripts a month. Three a week.
Storyboard and shot notes on every one. And the engine learns: the data re-ranks the idea bank and the hooks each month, so month 3 scripts beat month 1 scripts.
